Summary

The domain BRUD.COM was stolen in August 2019 through an unauthorized inter-registrar transfer from GoDaddy to Openprovider.

GoDaddy failed to stop the transfer, refused to reverse it, and later regained registrar control in 2022 without restoring ownership.
